Let me try to explain the difference between paddings, margins and borders.

Padding provides space around the element.
Borders simply wrap the element. A border can wrap immediately around an element, or it may look further away from an element if more padding has been applied to the element.
Margins are on the outside. Think of the margins you use on a piece of paper. These margins give space away from the edge of the paper.
